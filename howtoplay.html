How to Play

  1. Cards are mixed up.
  2. Laid in rows, face down.
  3. Turn over any two cards.
  4. If the two cards match, they stay.
  5. If they don't match, they turn back over.
  6. Remember what was on each card and where it was.
  7. The game is over when all the cards have been matched, or the timer has ran out.
